Apple Pie

Price: $12.95

Description

Betany received the recipe for this delicious apple pie from her grandmother. At the age of 5, she was already baking this all-American traditional apple pie at her home. As the recipe goes a long way back, it contains the most typical ingredients used for somany years to make this classic even one of our most popular pies.

This pie is avaiable every day and comes in 2 sizes. You can serve it cold or warm for 4 minutes in the microwave to give it an extra touch Your house will smell just like Bethany's shop.

This recipe has been used in our family for generations. We hope you enjoy this pie as we do. - Bethany

How we make this pie

  1. First, we combine sugar, flour and spices. We toss the apples with some lemon juice and add sugar
  2. We then create the bottom crust and fill that with apple mixture. We put some butter on that. We then roll remaining crust to fit top of pie.
  3. Then we beat the eggs white until we have a foamy mix. That is then brushed over the crust. We sprinkle some extra sugar.
  4. Then the pie goes into the oven at 3600 for 35 minutes. When ready, we let it cool on a wire rack.
